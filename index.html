Simple, in-browser text encryption for Chrome.

How-To

Getting started with Mummify is easy. Download and install the Chrome Extension below. Once installed, generate your set of keys below. Anyone can encrypt text using your public key, but only you are able to decrypt using your private key. Simply highlight any text, and it will copied into your clip-board or directly translated.

About This Project.

Mummify was created by a team of University students in less than 24 hours at nwHacks, the largest Hackathon in Western Canada. The team learned how to create a Chrome-extension and build much of the application on-the-fly. The application was made with privacy in mind, and providing users with an easy-to-use solution for encryption of virtually anything.
